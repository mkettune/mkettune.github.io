About Me

I'm a Senior Research Scientist at NVIDIA based in Helsinki, Finland. My goal is to enable real-time rendering of all 3D graphics at the highest quality, regardless of the complexity of light interactions. I leverage and invent advanced mathematics and statistical theory. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Aalto University and an M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Helsinki.
